# 🛡️ VidIQ — Safety Guidelines

## Content Safety Principles

### 1. User-Uploaded Content

**Accept:**
- General video content (sports, cooking, travel, education, entertainment)
- Professional content (business, presentations, tutorials)
- Personal content (home videos, events, celebrations)

**Reject Gracefully:**
- Explicit/adult content → "This video type is not supported."
- Violent content → "This video contains content we cannot analyze."
- Copyrighted full-length media → "Please use shorter clips for analysis."

### 2. AI Output Safety

**Always:**
- Generate factual, objective descriptions
- Describe what is physically visible
- Use professional, neutral language
- Note uncertainty when detection confidence is low

**Never:**
- Make assumptions about people's race, religion, or personal identity
- Generate offensive or discriminatory descriptions
- Speculate about illegal activities
- Identify specific individuals by name (unless clearly labeled in video)

---

## Data Privacy

### Video Handling
- Videos are stored temporarily for processing only
- Auto-delete uploaded videos after 30 days
- Analysis text is retained (not the video itself)
- Users can delete their data at any time

### Personal Information
- No facial recognition database
- No biometric data storage
- No cross-referencing of people across analyses
- No sharing of user data with third parties

### GDPR Compliance
- Full data export on request
- Complete deletion on request
- Clear privacy policy
- Cookie consent for analytics
- Right to be forgotten

| Limit                  | Value          | Reason                    |

| Max video duration     | 10 min (free)  | Cost control              |
