# 🎨 VidIQ — Brand Guidelines

## Name

**VidIQ** — Video Intelligence Query. Short, memorable, instantly communicates purpose.

**Alternatives considered:** FrameView, SceneScope, VideoLens, TimeCut, FrameNarrative

---

## Tagline

> **"See what your video really shows."**

**Secondary taglines:**
- "Every frame tells a story."
- "Timestamped intelligence for any video."
- "Your video, decoded."

---

## Tone of Voice

### The Personality
VidIQ speaks like a **brilliant, approachable analyst** — precise yet conversational. Think: documentary narrator meets tech-savvy friend.

### Tone Principles

| Principle       | Do                                    | Don't                              |
|-----------------|---------------------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| **Clear**       | Precise descriptions, structured data | Vague, ambiguous language           |
| **Smart**       | Insightful observations, context      | Over-technical jargon               |
| **Engaging**    | Narrative flow, scene storytelling    | Dry, clinical output                |
| **Confident**   | Definitive scene descriptions         | "Maybe," "possibly," uncertain      |
| **Accessible**  | Consumer-friendly language            | Academic, research-paper tone       |

### Example Copy

**Loading screens:**
- "Decoding your video frame by frame…"
- "Teaching AI to watch TV…"
- "Spotting things you missed…"
- "Building your scene timeline…"

**Analysis results:**
- "14 distinct scenes detected across 44 seconds"
- "3 brand logos identified: ENEOS, Inditech, KUGAY"
- "Key action: synchronized tandem drift at 00:13"

## Logo Concept

- Film strip / play button hybrid mark
- Bold, geometric sans-serif wordmark
- Works in both full color and single-color
- Minimum size: 24px height

---

## Responsible Messaging

### Always:
- Frame as a productivity / analysis tool
- Use terms like "video intelligence," "scene analysis," "visual understanding"
- Respect copyright in examples

### Never:
- Encourage surveillance usage
- Claim perfect accuracy
- Process content without user consent
- Store personally identifiable information from videos
